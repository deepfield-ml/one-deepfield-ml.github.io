DeepField E2: Lightweight Jersey Digit Recognition

Our latest project, DeepField E2, is a lightweight mixture-of-experts engine for jersey digit recognition, achieved through knowledge distillation. It's designed for resource-constrained environments like edge devices.

Key Features:

  • High Accuracy: Achieves 94.6% test accuracy.
  • Lightweight: Only ~16MB in size.
  • Efficient: Reduces computational overhead by 78% compared to traditional models.
  • Knowledge Distillation: Leverages knowledge from a ResNet-34 teacher model to an EfficientNet-B0 student architecture.
