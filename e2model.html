DeepField E2: A Lightweight Mixture-of-Experts

DeepField E2 is a jersey digit recognition engine designed for efficiency and accuracy.

Architecture

E2 uses a knowledge distillation approach:

  • Teacher Model: ResNet-34 (provides "soft labels").
  • Student Model: EfficientNet-B0 backbone with a modified classification layer.

Key Techniques

  • Knowledge Distillation: Transfers knowledge from the teacher model to the student model, improving accuracy and reducing size.
  • Compound Scaling: Balances depth, width, and resolution for optimal performance.
  • Hybrid Loss Function: Combines cross-entropy and Kullback-Leibler (KL) divergence.
  • Data Augmentation: Robust data augmentation techniques to improve generalization.
